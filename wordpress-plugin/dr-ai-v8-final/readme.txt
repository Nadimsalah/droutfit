== Description ==

DrOutfit is the most advanced and ultra-safe AI Virtual Try-On solution for WooCommerce. Empower your customers to visualize themselves in your products, reducing returns by up to 40% and boosting conversion rates instantly.

Our deep-learning engine handles body shapes, lighting, and textures with high fidelity, providing a seamless shopping experience directly on your product pages.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `droutfit-virtual-try-on` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to the DrOutfit Settings menu in your dashboard.
4. Log in with your DrOutfit account or enter your Access Token.
5. The "Virtual Try-On" button will automatically appear on your WooCommerce product pages.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does it work with any theme? =
Yes, DrOutfit is designed to be theme-agnostic. We inject our logic directly into the WooCommerce hooks.

= Is it mobile-friendly? =
Absolutely. The AI Virtual Try-On interface is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices.

= How do I synchronize my products? =
Once the plugin is installed, you can enable try-on for specific products directly from the WooCommerce product editor.
